# CivicPulse — Demo Script for Judges

**Time: 3 minutes**

---

## Opening Line (15 sec)
"90% of civic issues in Indian cities are never formally reported. People complain on Twitter, on WhatsApp — but nobody fills the government form. CivicPulse doesn't wait for citizens to report. The AI agent already knows."

---

## Live Demo Steps

### Step 1 — Show the map (30 sec)
Open the app. Show the map with markers in different colors.
> "These issues weren't reported by any citizen. Our Discovery Agent found them automatically from 847 social media signals in the last 6 hours. Yellow = just discovered, Orange = confirmed by citizens, Blue = already escalated to the government."

### Step 2 — Click an issue (30 sec)
Click on a DISCOVERED issue. Show the source signals.
> "This streetlight issue on Koramangala 5th block was found from 4 separate Twitter complaints. The agent clustered them geographically, confirmed they're about the same location, and surfaced it with 60% confidence."

### Step 3 — Citizen confirms (45 sec)
Click "+ Confirm", upload a photo (prepare one in advance), submit.
> "Now watch — I tap Confirm, upload a photo, and Gemini Vision validates it matches a streetlight issue. Confidence just jumped to 75%. When 5 citizens do this, Agent 3 fires."

### Step 4 — Trigger escalation (45 sec)
Click "Run Escalation" in the header (or pre-stage an issue with 5 confirmations).
> "We've hit the threshold. Watch what happens. Agent 3 calls Gemini — identifies this is a BESCOM issue, not BBMP. Drafts a formal letter. Sends it. The issue is now ESCALATED."

Show the escalation log with the letter content.
> "12 citizens confirmed this. The letter says that. It has GPS coordinates for the field team. It requests action within 7 days. This took 0 seconds of human effort."

### Step 5 — Impact dashboard (15 sec)
Point to the stats.
> "In 24 hours: 23 issues discovered, 9 escalated to the right authorities, 2 resolved. Zero manual reporting."

---

## Closing Line (15 sec)
"CivicPulse doesn't build a better form. It makes the form irrelevant. The agent already knows. The citizen confirms. The authority acts."
